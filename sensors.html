Sensor Information

🌿 MQ131 (Ozone Sensor)

🔹 **Detects ozone (O₃) in the air** and is used for **air quality monitoring**.

🔹 **Parameters:** Ozone concentration (ppm).

🔹 **Functionality:** Uses a gas-sensitive semiconductor to detect ozone levels.

🔹 **Application:** Pollution control, industrial safety.

💨 MH-Z19 CO₂ Sensor

🔹 Uses **NDIR technology** to measure **carbon dioxide (CO₂) levels**.

🔹 **Parameters:** CO₂ concentration (ppm), temperature.

🔹 **Functionality:** Infrared absorption detects CO₂ levels accurately.

🔹 **Application:** Indoor air quality monitoring, HVAC systems.

🌫️ PMS (Particulate Matter Sensor)

🔹 Detects **PM1.0, PM2.5, and PM10 particles** in the air.

🔹 **Parameters:** PM1.0, PM2.5, PM10 concentrations (µg/m³).

🔹 **Functionality:** Uses a laser-based scattering method to measure particle density.

🔹 **Application:** Air pollution measurement, environmental studies.

⚗️ pH Sensor

🔹 Measures **the acidity or alkalinity of a solution**.

🔹 **Parameters:** pH value (0-14).

🔹 **Functionality:** Glass electrode detects hydrogen ion activity.

🔹 **Application:** Water quality testing, agriculture, aquariums.

💧 Conductivity Sensor

🔹 Measures **water’s ability to conduct electricity**, indicating purity.

🔹 **Parameters:** Electrical conductivity (µS/cm).

🔹 **Functionality:** Measures ion concentration in water using electrode plates.

🔹 **Application:** Water treatment, environmental monitoring.

🌊 Turbidity Sensor

🔹 Measures **water clarity** by detecting suspended particles.

🔹 **Parameters:** Turbidity level (NTU - Nephelometric Turbidity Units).

🔹 **Functionality:** Uses an optical sensor to measure light scattering.

🔹 **Application:** Water treatment, wastewater management.
